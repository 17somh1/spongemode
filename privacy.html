SpOnGeMoDe Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

SpOnGeMoDe does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All text transformations happen locally within your browser. The extension does not send any information to external servers, does not use analytics or cookies, and does not track user activity or browsing history.

Permissions such as activeTab, scripting, storage, and contextMenus are used solely to enable the extension’s core functionality — transforming text into mocking case and remembering your local settings.

If you have any questions, you can contact the developer at marksohangfung@gmail.com.
